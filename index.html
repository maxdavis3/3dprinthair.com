HAIR TOOL MATERIALS

Materials for Hair Products

Hair tools need to be light, strong, heat-tolerant, and smooth enough not to snag — our material selection covers all requirements.

  • ● Nylon PA12 — lightweight, flexible, smooth surface
  • ● ASA — heat-tolerant, UV-stable for salon environments
  • ● TPU 85A — flexible clip and grip applications
  • ● Polycarbonate — high-impact professional tool housing
  • ● Electroless nickel-coated polymer for premium aesthetics
  • ● Silicone — heat-resistant diffuser seal components
